# Forge — Google Play Store Listing

## App Title (max 30 chars)
Forge: Social App for Gamers

## Short Description (max 80 chars)
Social app for gamers: share clips, discover games & build your community.

## Long Description (max 4000 chars)

Forge is the gaming social network built for gamers — share your gaming moments, discover new titles, and connect with players across every gaming platform.

Whether you're into console, PC, or mobile gaming, Forge is your home for everything gaming. Build your profile, share clips and reviews, follow other gamers, and join communities built around the games you love.

FEATURES

🎮 Build Your Gamer Profile
Show off your gaming platforms, social handles, game library (Recently Played, Favorites, Wishlist, Library), and the communities you're part of.

📸 Share Your World
Post gaming moments, reviews, and clips. Tag the games you're playing and the friends you're playing with.

🌐 Follow Across Platforms
Your feed can pull in posts from real gamers and top gaming media like IGN, Kotaku, PC Gamer, Eurogamer, Rock Paper Shotgun, GameSpot, and more — all in one place.

🔍 Explore & Discover
Browse trending gaming posts, find new players to follow, search games from a catalog of thousands, and participate in gaming communities built around the games and topics you love.

👥 Groups & Communities
Join open groups, request access to invite-only groups, or build your own gaming community. Groups have their own feeds, profiles, and game lists.

🔒 Safe & Inclusive
Forge is built for every kind of gamer. You're in control of what you see. LGBTQ+ voices, marginalized communities, and all kinds of gamers are welcome here.

💡 Game Insights Wiki
Community-sourced knowledge for your favorite games. Ask questions, submit discoveries, and vote on insights from other gamers.


COMING SOON

• Listicles — a new way to talk about games
• Game library sync with Steam, PlayStation & Xbox
• Games hosted on Forge
• iOS app (web version available now at forge-social.app)


BETA PROGRAM

Forge is currently in open beta. We're building in public, listening closely, and updating regularly. Found a bug or have a feature idea? We want to hear from you.
